FAQs

Can merchants set their own pricing?
Yes, merchants have full control over their product pricing. Our platform only manages the affiliate commission structure.
How are affiliate commissions calculated?
Affiliates earn a commission from each sale they generate. Our platform charges a commission on the affiliate's share, ranging from 45% for new affiliates to 20% for high-volume sales.
When do affiliates receive their commission?
Commissions are processed after the merchant's standard refund period, which is usually 7 days. If a sale is refunded within this period, no commission is paid to the affiliate.
What happens if a sale is refunded?
If a sale is refunded within the merchant's refund policy window (usually 7 days), the affiliate will not receive any commission for that sale.
